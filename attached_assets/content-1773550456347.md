**Valin Draco** was a [male](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Gender/Legends "Gender/Legends") [Human](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Human/Legends "Human/Legends") [Jedi Knight](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jedi_Knight/Legends "Jedi Knight/Legends") who was the student of [Jedi Master](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jedi_Master/Legends "Jedi Master/Legends") [Denia](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Denia "Denia") during the waning [years](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Standard_year/Legends "Standard year/Legends") of the [Galactic Republic](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Galactic_Republic/Legends "Galactic Republic/Legends"). Following the outbreak of the [Clone Wars](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Clone_Wars/Legends "Clone Wars/Legends"), he fought for the Republic against the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems/Legends "Confederacy of Independent Systems/Legends"). However, his resolve to destroy the Confederacy drew him towards the [dark side of the Force](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force/Legends "Dark side of the Force/Legends"), and after he was almost [killed](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Death/Legends "Death/Legends") during the [Battle of Parein II 4](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_Parein_II_4/Legends "Battle of Parein II 4/Legends"), he fell fully into its grip.

Following the [creation](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Declaration_of_a_New_Order "Declaration of a New Order") of the [Galactic Empire](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Galactic_Empire/Legends "Galactic Empire/Legends"), Draco allied himself with it and became a member of the [Inquisitorius](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Inquisitorius/Legends "Inquisitorius/Legends"), an order of [Dark Side Adepts](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_side_adept/Legends "Dark side adept/Legends") that served the [New Order](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/New_Order/Legends "New Order/Legends"). He adapted well to his new position and soon formulated a plan to try and find evidence that [Senator](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Senator/Legends "Senator/Legends") [Bail Organa](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bail_Prestor_Organa/Legends "Bail Prestor Organa/Legends") had betrayed the Empire. Draco suspected that Organa was the leader of a [resistance group](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Alderaanian_Resistance "Alderaanian Resistance") based on [Alderaan](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Alderaan/Legends "Alderaan/Legends"), and he helped [Admiral](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Admiral/Legends "Admiral/Legends") [Gilder Varth](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Gilder_Varth "Gilder Varth") to infiltrate the movement. He also became part of the [Sarlacc Project](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Sarlacc_Project "Sarlacc Project"), a secret collaboration between the Inquisitorius and the [Imperial Navy](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Navy/Legends "Imperial Navy/Legends") to build a [Super Star Destroyer prototype](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Super_Star_Destroyer_prototype "Super Star Destroyer prototype").

When a powerful [Force nexus](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Vergence/Legends "Vergence/Legends") began to develop on the [planet](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Planet/Legends "Planet/Legends") [Almas](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Almas "Almas"), Draco was sent to investigate it. In the ruins of the [Almas Academy](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Almas_Academy "Almas Academy"), a [Jedi academy](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jedi_academy "Jedi academy") that was [destroyed](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Destruction_of_the_Almas_Academy "Destruction of the Almas Academy") during the Clone Wars, he discovered and learned [Force](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/The_Force/Legends "The Force/Legends") techniques from a [Sith holocron](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Darth_Rivan%27s_holocron "Darth Rivan's holocron") that was constructed by the ancient [Sith Lord](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Sith_Lord/Legends "Sith Lord/Legends") [Darth Rivan](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Darth_Rivan "Darth Rivan"). However, Draco was seriously injured after an [encounter](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mission_to_Almas "Mission to Almas") with the [Alderaanian](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Alderaanian/Legends "Alderaanian/Legends") Resistance, and had to be fitted with extensive [cybernetics](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Cybernetics/Legends "Cybernetics/Legends"). He gained revenge by [attacking](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_the_Resurgence "Battle of the Resurgence") a Resistance [task force](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Task_force/Legends "Task force/Legends") and capturing Denia, his former master. He took her to the [headquarters of the Inquisitorius](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Citadel_Inquisitorius "Citadel Inquisitorius") on [Prakith](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Prakith/Legends "Prakith/Legends"), a planet located in the [Deep Core](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Deep_Core/Legends "Deep Core/Legends"), and tortured her there. However, a rescue team traveled to Prakith to free her, and Denia sacrificed herself to weaken Draco, forcing him to flee to the planet [Byss](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Byss/Legends "Byss/Legends") where the [Super Star Destroyer](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Super_Star_Destroyer/Legends "Super Star Destroyer/Legends") prototype was under construction. There, Draco took command of a [Golan space defense platform](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Golan_space_defense_platform "Golan space defense platform") that was protecting the prototype and he was killed when the [platform](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Space_station/Legends "Space station/Legends") was [attacked](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_Byss "Battle of Byss") by the Alderaanian Resistance.

## Biography\[ [Edit section: Biography](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco?veaction=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Biography")\]

### Jedi Knight\[ [Edit section: Jedi Knight](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco?veaction=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Jedi Knight")\]

" _During the Clone Wars, the brave men and women I commanded called me the_ Valiant Dragon _._"―Valin Draco[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

Valin Draco was a [Human](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Human/Legends "Human/Legends")[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5) [male](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Gender/Legends "Gender/Legends")[\[4\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-The_Queen_of_Air_and_Darkness-4) who served in the [Jedi Order](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jedi_Order/Legends "Jedi Order/Legends") during the waning [years](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Standard_year/Legends "Standard year/Legends") of the [Galactic Republic](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Galactic_Republic/Legends "Galactic Republic/Legends"). Draco [trained](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jedi_training/Legends "Jedi training/Legends") as the [Padawan](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Padawan/Legends "Padawan/Legends") of [Jedi Master](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jedi_Master/Legends "Jedi Master/Legends") [Denia](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Denia "Denia").[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5) He fought during the [Clone Wars](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Clone_Wars/Legends "Clone Wars/Legends")[\[3\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-A_Wretched_Hive-3) and fully embraced his role as a [commander](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jedi_Commander/Legends "Jedi Commander/Legends") in the [Grand Army of the Republic](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Grand_Army_of_the_Republic/Legends "Grand Army of the Republic/Legends"), so much so that the people he commanded nicknamed him the "Valiant Dragon." However, his determination to defeat the [Separatists](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Confederacy_of_Independent_Systems/Legends "Confederacy of Independent Systems/Legends") gradually began to rise as the war progressed and drew him closer to the [dark side](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_side_of_the_Force/Legends "Dark side of the Force/Legends") of [the Force](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/The_Force/Legends "The Force/Legends"). Draco participated in the [Battle of Parein II 4](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_Parein_II_4/Legends "Battle of Parein II 4/Legends"), a battle fought between the Republic and the Confederacy on the [fourth moon](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Parein_II_4/Legends "Parein II 4/Legends") of the [planet](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Planet/Legends "Planet/Legends") [Parein II](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Parein_II/Legends "Parein II/Legends"),[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5) where he was under the command of [Jedi General](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jedi_General/Legends "Jedi General/Legends") [Sannen](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Sannen/Legends "Sannen/Legends"). However, the Republic forces were defeated,[\[9\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Lord_of_War-9) and Draco was injured and left for [dead](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Death/Legends "Death/Legends"). He believed that he had been abandoned, and his support of the Republic died. As he lay injured, he was attacked by a [nighthunter](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Maalraas "Maalraas"), a dangerous [Force-sensitive](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Force-sensitive/Legends "Force-sensitive/Legends") [species](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Species/Legends "Species/Legends") of predator that was thought to be extinct. Draco fought back, and the two almost killed each other. Eventually, he managed to subdue the creature, but not before it had bitten off his thumb and clamped its jaws around his [throat](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Throat/Legends "Throat/Legends"), causing extensive scarring. Rather than leave it to die, Draco nursed the hunter back to health, named it [Crant](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Crant "Crant"), and kept it as a pet.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

### Serving the Empire\[ [Edit section: Serving the Empire](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco?veaction=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Serving the Empire")\]

" _My name is Valin Draco, and I will find you and make you pay for meddling here. You may have bested that slimy Hutt, but I can assure you that the affections of an Inquisitor are not so gentle._"―Valin Draco[\[4\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-The_Queen_of_Air_and_Darkness-4)

[![Draco with his lightsaber ignited](<Base64-Image-Removed>)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/8/83/Draco_New.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20080306005019)

Draco with his lightsaber ignited

Draco somehow escaped Parein II 4 and allied himself with the [Galactic Empire](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Galactic_Empire/Legends "Galactic Empire/Legends"), the newly- [formed](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Declaration_of_a_New_Order "Declaration of a New Order") galactic government that had replaced the Republic, and joined the ranks of [Emperor](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Galactic_Emperor/Legends "Galactic Emperor/Legends") and [Dark Lord of the Sith](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith/Legends "Dark Lord of the Sith/Legends") [Palpatine's](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Palpatine/Legends "Palpatine/Legends") [Dark Side Adepts](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_side_adept/Legends "Dark side adept/Legends") as an [Inquisitor](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Inquisitorius/Legends "Inquisitorius/Legends"). He was fitted with a [cybernetic](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Cybernetics/Legends "Cybernetics/Legends") thumb to replace the one that Crant had bitten off, and he used his new Inquisitor tunic to hide his neck scars.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5) He was also given command of the [_Imperial I_-class Star Destroyer](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_I-class_Star_Destroyer/Legends "Imperial I-class Star Destroyer/Legends") _[Assiduous](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Assiduous "Assiduous")_, and had the [12th Squadron](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/12th_Squadron "12th Squadron") of [TIE/LN starfighters](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/TIE/ln_space_superiority_starfighter/Legends "TIE/ln space superiority starfighter/Legends") assigned to it at his personal request.[\[4\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-The_Queen_of_Air_and_Darkness-4) Draco also expanded his personal armory by constructing[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5) a [red](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Color/Legends "Color/Legends")-bladed [lightsaber](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber/Legends "Lightsaber/Legends")[\[10\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-CSWM-10) and acquiring a [Star Courier](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Courier/Legends "Star Courier/Legends").[\[8\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-SotG-8)

Draco soon found himself an apprentice in the form of the [Dark Jedi](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_Jedi/Legends "Dark Jedi/Legends") [Raik Muun](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Raik_Muun "Raik Muun"). Muun had held a grudge against the Jedi, but following the Order's [destruction](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Order_66/Legends "Order 66/Legends") at the end of the Clone Wars, she was prevented from having revenge against them. Muun had traveled to the [galactic](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/The_galaxy/Legends "The galaxy/Legends") [capital](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Capital/Legends "Capital/Legends") of [Coruscant](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Coruscant/Legends "Coruscant/Legends") to try and find those who had ordered the execution of the Jedi and cheated her out of her vengeance. However, things did not go as she expected, and she instead met Draco there. He saw great potential in her, and he asked her to join him. She accepted, as she felt that she had finally found someone to love her, and she accompanied him on various assignments. Around this time, Draco also aided the [Sith Lord](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Sith_Lord/Legends "Sith Lord/Legends") [Darth Vader](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Anakin_Skywalker/Legends "Anakin Skywalker/Legends") in [torturing](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Torture/Legends "Torture/Legends") the captured Jedi [Halagad Ventor](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Halagad_Ventor "Halagad Ventor"). The Sith Lord showed his gratitude by giving Draco a squad of [Noghri Death Commandos](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Noghri_Death_Commando "Noghri Death Commando") to use as his own.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

In circa [17 BBY](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/17_BBY/Legends "17 BBY/Legends"),[\[1\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Date-1) Draco conspired with [Admiral](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Admiral/Legends "Admiral/Legends") [Gilder Varth](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Gilder_Varth "Gilder Varth") of the [Imperial Navy](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Navy/Legends "Imperial Navy/Legends") to infiltrate the [Alderaanian Resistance](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Alderaanian_Resistance "Alderaanian Resistance"), intending for Varth to become a member of it. Their goal was to gain evidence that [Senator](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Senator/Legends "Senator/Legends") [Bail Organa](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bail_Prestor_Organa/Legends "Bail Prestor Organa/Legends") had betrayed the Empire and was secretly leading the Resistance. Draco had Varth display interest in joining the movement, and to ensure that no one suspected that Varth was still loyal to the Empire, he had the Admiral imprisoned in a [facility](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Vanishing_Place "Vanishing Place") on the [Outer Rim](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Outer_Rim_Territories/Legends "Outer Rim Territories/Legends") planet [Felucia](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Felucia/Legends "Felucia/Legends").[\[11\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-The_Traitor's_Gambit-11) Draco wanted the Resistance to free Varth, and limited the number of Imperial troops stationed at the facility, under guise of trying to keep its existence secret. The Resistance took the bait and [sent](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mission_to_Felucia_(Imperial_Period) "Mission to Felucia (Imperial Period)") a team of operatives to free Varth.[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) To keep up the pretense of Varth's defection, Draco sent a message to the prison while the team were still there, stating that he would be arriving in the [system](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Felucia_system/Legends "Felucia system/Legends") aboard the _Assiduous_ to take custody of Varth, and wanted interrogation chemicals to be administered to the Admiral on his arrival. Everything went according to plan, and Varth took up a post in the Resistance.[\[11\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-The_Traitor's_Gambit-11)

Around this [time](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Time/Legends "Time/Legends"), Draco became involved with the [Sarlacc Project](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Sarlacc_Project "Sarlacc Project"), the secret construction of a [Super Star Destroyer prototype](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Super_Star_Destroyer_prototype "Super Star Destroyer prototype") to serve as a [flagship](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Flagship/Legends "Flagship/Legends") for the Imperial [Navy](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Navy/Legends "Navy/Legends").[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) He oversaw a deal made with the [Hutt](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Hutt/Legends "Hutt/Legends") [Darga Jiramma Mionne](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Darga_Jiramma_Mionne "Darga Jiramma Mionne"), who agreed to supply [tibanna gas](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tibanna/Legends "Tibanna/Legends") for the project in return for [Nazren](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Nazren "Nazren") [slaves](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Slavery/Legends "Slavery/Legends"). [Igren Demos](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Igren_Demos "Igren Demos"), the Hutt's [majordomo](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Majordomo/Legends "Majordomo/Legends"), began to secretly contact Draco, as he hoped that he could use his alliance with the Empire to take control of Darga's organization. Draco's former master, Denia, had been held captive in [Darga's palace](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Darga_the_Hutt%27s_palace "Darga the Hutt's palace") on the planet [Cato Neimoidia](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Cato_Neimoidia/Legends "Cato Neimoidia/Legends") since the Clone Wars, after being captured by the Separatists and placed in an artificial coma. When she was reawakened[\[3\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-A_Wretched_Hive-3) and [freed](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_Darga%27s_palace "Battle of Darga's palace") by an [Alderaanian](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Alderaanian/Legends "Alderaanian/Legends") Resistance team, Darga fled the palace, and Demos contacted Draco[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) via [HoloNet](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/HoloNet/Legends "HoloNet/Legends"). The Majordomo told him that he had cornered Denia, and wanted to trade her in return for the Inquisitor's assurance that Darga would never return to Cato Neimoidia. Draco agreed, and said that he would travel there aboard the _Assiduous_, but he threatened Demos that he would not be pleased with him if he failed to hand over Denia.[\[3\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-A_Wretched_Hive-3) However, the Jedi Master successfully escaped[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) before Draco arrived.[\[3\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-A_Wretched_Hive-3)

Darga relocated to the gas planet [Bespin](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bespin/Legends "Bespin/Legends"),[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) and Draco traveled there aboard the _Assiduous_ to oversee the transfer of funds to the Hutt—which would occur in secret during the [Cloud City Sabacc Tournament](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Cloud_City_Sabacc_Tournament "Cloud City Sabacc Tournament").[\[4\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-The_Queen_of_Air_and_Darkness-4) The Alderaanian Resistance team had tracked Darga to Bespin and infiltrated the tournament, discovering the truth about the deal.[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) In order to cover up the arrangement, Draco used [slave circuitry](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Slave_circuit "Slave circuit") to take control of a [Tibanna gas hauler](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tibanna_gas_hauler "Tibanna gas hauler"), and tried to crash it into the slave ship _[Shackles of Nizon](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Shackles_of_Nizon "Shackles of Nizon")_, which would kill all the slaves inside[\[4\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-The_Queen_of_Air_and_Darkness-4) and destroy all evidence of the deal. The team commandeered several [Z-95 Headhunters](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Z-95_Headhunter/Legends "Z-95 Headhunter/Legends") and managed to prevent this from happening.[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) While they were there, Draco felt through the Force a presence that he had not felt since he was with Denia, and he suspected that this was the same team that had rescued her on Cato Neimoidia.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5) As the team flew out of the [system](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bespin_system/Legends "Bespin system/Legends"), Draco sent a transmission to them, saying that he would find them and make them pay for what they had done.[\[4\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-The_Queen_of_Air_and_Darkness-4)

### Investigating the Almas Academy\[ [Edit section: Investigating the Almas Academy](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco?veaction=edit&section=4 "Edit section: Investigating the Almas Academy")\]

" _Surrender yourselves to the Empire. I promise your treatment will be gentler, and you might even be given the chance to serve me._"―Valin Draco[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

Several [months](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Standard_month/Legends "Standard month/Legends") later,[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5) Draco traveled on a mission to [Almas](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Almas "Almas"), a planet located in the [Cularin system](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Cularin_system/Legends "Cularin system/Legends").[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) Raik Muun[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5) and a contingent of Imperial troops,[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) including Draco's [Noghri](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Noghri/Legends "Noghri/Legends"), were assigned to accompany him. In addition, Draco brought a detachment of the elite [clone shadow troopers](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Clone_shadow_trooper/Legends "Clone shadow trooper/Legends"), as he believed that if they were good enough to guard the [Emperor's](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Emperor/Legends "Emperor/Legends") storehouse on the planet [Utapau](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Utapau/Legends "Utapau/Legends"), then they were good enough to guard him. He had been sent to investigate the growing power of a [Force nexus](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Vergence/Legends "Vergence/Legends") that was centered around the [Almas Academy](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Almas_Academy "Almas Academy"), a former [Jedi academy](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jedi_academy "Jedi academy"), and also because he had learned from torturing [Force-sensitive](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Force-sensitive/Legends "Force-sensitive/Legends") prisoners that there was a [Sith holocron](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Sith_holocron/Legends "Sith holocron/Legends") there.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

Draco arrived in the system aboard the _Assiduous_, which was accompanied by the [_Lictor_-class dungeon ship](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Lictor-class_dungeon_ship "Lictor-class dungeon ship") [_Revelator_](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Revelator_(Lictor-class) "Revelator (Lictor-class)"). He first searched the system for any Jedi who had survived the [destruction of the Almas Academy](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Destruction_of_the_Almas_Academy "Destruction of the Almas Academy") at the end of the Clone Wars, but failed to locate any. Following this, he headed for Almas. Both his ships were too large to traverse the system's [comet field](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Cularin_comet_cloud "Cularin comet cloud"), and he and his entourage were forced to make the journey to Almas in a [_Theta_-class shuttle](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Theta-class_T-2c_shuttle/Legends "Theta-class T-2c shuttle/Legends"), and landed in the remains of the [Forard](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Forard "Forard") [spaceport](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Spaceport/Legends "Spaceport/Legends"). Draco arrived in the academy ruins, and was immediately entranced by an intoxicating surge of dark side energy. Attempting to locate the source of this power, he went into the caves beneath the academy, where he found the [holocron](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Darth_Rivan%27s_holocron "Darth Rivan's holocron") of [Darth Rivan](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Darth_Rivan "Darth Rivan"), a [Dark Lord of the Sith](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_Lord_of_the_Sith/Legends "Dark Lord of the Sith/Legends") who had ruled Almas over a thousand years earlier. The growing dark side energy on Almas had suffused the [holocron](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Holocron/Legends "Holocron/Legends") and given it greater power, which enabled the holocron gatekeeper to take on a life of his or her own and believe himself or herself to be the spirit of Rivan.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

However, Draco and his entourage were not alone in the Academy. Jedi Master [Vhiin Thorla](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Vhiin_Thorla "Vhiin Thorla") had already arrived there along with his Padawan, [Dorv'Tilsta](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dorv%27Tilsta "Dorv'Tilsta"), in search of any Jedi survivors of Order 66. They encountered Draco and Raik Muun, and engaged them in a [lightsaber duel](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Duel_in_the_Almas_Academy "Duel in the Almas Academy"). Draco threatened Thorla that he would turn Tilsta to the dark side if he refused to fight, and Muun used the Force to coerce the young Padawan into a murderous rage, directed at his Master. Thorla was forced to strike his apprentice down, but the grief the Jedi Master suffered in doing so allowed Draco to easily capture him. The Inquisitor imprisoned Thorla in a Force-annulling [Universal Energy Cage](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Universal_Energy_Cage "Universal Energy Cage"), and left Crant to guard it. Draco had plans for Thorla, and he conspired with the Rivan gatekeeper in the hopes of turning the Jedi Master to the dark side.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

Draco took Rivan's holocron to a chamber beneath the Academy's archive room, where he studied it and learned some of the Sith techniques that Rivan had known. Under the influence of the gatekeeper, Draco left various traps and creatures around the academy to discourage intruders. The holocron persuaded him to release two parasitic [Guardian Spirits](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Guardian_Spirit "Guardian Spirit") in the Academy's library. He also used techniques that the holocron had shown him to create a [Force illusion](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mind_trick/Legends "Mind trick/Legends") in a room that made anyone who entered the room fall asleep and come under Draco's influence. They would then have a Force-induced nightmare about being in a room in the Academy that resembled one they had previously been in, and while seeming familiar, it did not seem real. They would begin to feel claustrophobic and disorientated, making them despair and allowing Draco—through the Force—to sap their moral strength.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

In the same room, Draco created a [K'kayeh](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/K%27kayeh_dragon "K'kayeh dragon") [dreambeast](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dreambeast "Dreambeast") through the Force, using another technique that the Rivan gatekeeper had shown him, similar to those used to create [Sith Dopplegängers](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Doppleg%C3%A4nger "Dopplegänger"). The dreambeast was an illusion that resembled a larger K'kayeh that was hidden beneath the Academy, to which it was linked through the Force. The holocron also revealed to him the location of the [Qornah Holocron](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Qornah_Holocron "Qornah Holocron"), a holocron made by Jedi Master [Qornah](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Qornah "Qornah"), which was concealed inside a [bronzium](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bronzium/Legends "Bronzium/Legends") bust of the Jedi Master in the Academy's library. Draco sliced the bust in half and retrieved the holocron, keeping it strapped to his wrist from then on. At some point, Draco hired the [pirates](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Pirate/Legends "Pirate/Legends") of the [Red Fury Brotherhood](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Red_Fury_Brotherhood/Legends "Red Fury Brotherhood/Legends") to prevent outsiders from landing on Almas by having their ships patrol the planet's orbit.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

Meanwhile, Master Denia sent the Alderaanian Resistance team to the academy on a [mission](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mission_to_Almas "Mission to Almas") to recover the Qornah Holocron.[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) They found Thorla's prison and killed Crant, which Draco felt through the Force. He continued to study both Rivan's and Qornah's holocrons, but was interrupted from his work when he was confronted by the Resistance team. Draco realized that the team must have been formidable foes, as they had survived all the traps and creatures that he had left in the academy. He urged them to surrender, but they refused, which instigated a fight between them, Raik Muun, and Draco's team of Shadow troopers. The Rivan gatekeeper used its Force connection with Draco to provide him with advanced Sith fighting techniques that Rivan had known. This turned the color of Draco's [eyes](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Eye/Legends "Eye/Legends") to a golden hue as he used the techniques against the Resistance fighters.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

Draco drew his lightsaber and then used [Force rage](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Enrage "Force Enrage") to enhance his Force power. The Inquisitor moved to confront them, and once he was within range, he attacked them with his saber. However, the team was able to use the Force to sever the gatekeeper's connection to the holocron, which caused it to dissipate and overload. As the shadow troopers and Muun were incapacitated, Draco became desperate to survive, due to the fact that the loss of the holocron had weakened his power. He began to lose his resolve, and he fled back to the entrance of a large cavern. However, the gatekeeper had planned to summon a K'kayeh dragon if it lost control of Draco, and a dragon emerged from the depths of the academy as Draco fled further back to a bridge formation. Draco used the Force to [control](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Animal_bond "Animal bond") the dragon, preventing it from devouring him, and then threatened to destroy the Qornah Holocron. He held it over the edge of the chasm while he fired [Force lightning](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Force_lightning/Legends "Force lightning/Legends") from his hands at the team. In turn, the team attacked Draco again, causing him to drop the holocron on the ground, from where it tumbled over the edge into the abyss.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

### The capture of Denia\[ [Edit section: The capture of Denia](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco?veaction=edit&section=5 "Edit section: The capture of Denia")\]

[![Denia, Draco's former Jedi Master, whom he captured.](<Base64-Image-Removed>)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/f/fa/Denia.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20080116063904)

Denia, Draco's former Jedi Master, whom he captured.

" _If the past is any indication, Draco will try to break her—it is only a matter of time._"―Bail Organa, to members of the Alderaanian Resistance[\[12\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-TGoA-12)

Draco somehow survived the encounter, and was fitted with extensive cybernetic enhancements,[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) including a cybernetic right eye.[\[6\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Sword_of_the_Empire-6) To hide these, he began to wear a black face-plate with a red optic center.[\[13\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-ARoW-13) He posted a bounty on the Resistance team and sent the [Zabrak](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Zabrak/Legends "Zabrak/Legends") [Vril Vrakth](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Vril_Vrakth "Vril Vrakth") to collect it. Vrakth tracked them to [Nizon](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Nizon "Nizon"), a planet located in the [Centares system](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Centares_system/Legends "Centares system/Legends"), and hoped to please Draco by bringing them to him. However, he failed and was killed during the [Battle of Nizon](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_Nizon "Battle of Nizon").[\[14\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-TFtS-14) A few weeks later, Gilder Varth sent Draco the coordinates of the location of an Alderaanian Resistance [task force](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Task_force/Legends "Task force/Legends") that was waiting near Coruscant. Draco led several [_Imperial_-class Star Destroyers](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial-class_Star_Destroyer/Legends "Imperial-class Star Destroyer/Legends") in an attack on the task force,[\[13\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-ARoW-13) and was among an Imperial boarding party that landed on the Alderaanian Resistance [Nebulon-B frigate](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/EF76_Nebulon-B_escort_frigate/Legends "EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate/Legends") [_Resurgence_](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Resurgence_(EF76_Nebulon-B_escort_frigate) "Resurgence (EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate)"),[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) which had been commanding the flotilla.[\[13\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-ARoW-13) Accompanying him were three other Inquisitors who had been sent to help him, although they were unhappy about being pulled from their duties and placed under his command. Aboard the vessel, Draco quickly captured Master Denia, and then planned to leave with her in his custody.[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7)

While Denia was being taken to a shuttle, the Alderaanian Resistance team that Draco had encountered on Almas arrived. He paid them little heed, and hurried aboard the shuttle where Denia had been taken, which launched moments later.[\[13\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-ARoW-13) He transferred Denia to his [Star Courier](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Courier/Legends "Star Courier/Legends"),[\[12\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-TGoA-12) and eventually made his way to the [Citadel Inquisitorius](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Citadel_Inquisitorius "Citadel Inquisitorius") on the planet [Prakith](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Prakith/Legends "Prakith/Legends") in the [Deep Core](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Deep_Core/Legends "Deep Core/Legends"), the headquarters of the Inquisitorius.[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) He took her to the Citadel's torture chamber, an octagonal-shaped room located high up in the building,[\[6\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Sword_of_the_Empire-6) where he began to torture her. However, she resisted him, and after a few months, he had still not broken her mind. This made his superiors impatient, and Draco began to use more brutal techniques on her.[\[12\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-TGoA-12)

Draco's old foes, the Alderaanian Resistance team, traveled to Prakith to try and free Denia. They infiltrated the Citadel and [confronted](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mission_to_Prakith_(Imperial_Period) "Mission to Prakith (Imperial Period)") Draco[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) in the torture chamber, where he was torturing his former Master with the help of two [T0-D interrogation droids](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/T0-D_interrogation_droid "T0-D interrogation droid"). He immediately attacked them and initially fought defensively, using an examination table to give him cover while he used the Force to try and knock them over and disarm them. Afterward, he engaged them in melee combat. He seemed unbeatable and it appeared that he was about to defeat the agents. However, Denia used her last reserves of strength to [grab](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Telekinesis/Legends "Telekinesis/Legends") Draco with the Force. The energy from her attack reflected back on to both of them, killing Denia and weakening Draco. It now seemed that the Alderaanians were going to win, so he turned and fled the room. He ran to a landing platform and left the Citadel[\[6\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Sword_of_the_Empire-6) aboard his personal shuttle.[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7)

### Confrontation on Byss\[ [Edit section: Confrontation on Byss](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco?veaction=edit&section=6 "Edit section: Confrontation on Byss")\]

" _Had I known, when our paths crossed so long ago on Almas, that you would become such a thorn in the Empire's side, I would have had the planet razed from orbit. Yet I did not, and here we stand. If you throw down your weapons now, I guarantee you a quick and painless death. As you can see, I have been looking forward to this meeting for some time now._"―Valin Draco, to agents of the Alderaanian Resistance[\[2\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-JOTS-2)

Draco traveled to the planet [Byss](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Byss/Legends "Byss/Legends"), where the [Super Star Destroyer](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Super_Star_Destroyer/Legends "Super Star Destroyer/Legends") prototype was under construction,[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) as his meditations revealed to him that his final confrontation with the Alderaanian agents would take place there. With Varth's approval, Draco made himself in charge of a [Golan space defense platform](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Golan_space_defense_platform "Golan space defense platform") that was being used to protect the [Star Destroyer](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Destroyer/Legends "Star Destroyer/Legends") and hold it in place with [tractor beams](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tractor_beam/Legends "Tractor beam/Legends"). However, Draco chose not to tell Varth about his visions, as he planned to wait for Varth to fail at defending the Star Destroyer, then step in, save the Star Destroyer, and take control of the Sarlacc Project and it's support [fleet](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Fleet/Legends "Fleet/Legends"). While he waited for the Alderaanian Resistance to attack, Draco stored his Star Courier in [Docking Bay](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Hangar/Legends "Hangar/Legends") 616 of the Golan platform and was also assigned two [Imperial Shadow Guards](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Emperor%27s_Shadow_Guard "Emperor's Shadow Guard") to serve him, as part of a training mission for them.[\[2\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-JOTS-2)

Draco's prediction proved to be correct and an Alderaanian Resistance fleet [attacked](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_of_Byss "Battle of Byss") Byss, intent on destroying the prototype. During the battle, Draco's old adversaries, the team of Resistance agents that he had encountered on Almas and Prakith, boarded the Golan station and planned to use it's weapons to attack the Super Star Destroyer. The Inquisitor waited for them on the station's auxiliary command bridge, along with a complement of stormtroopers and both of his Shadow Guards. He ignited his lightsaber and allowed the agents to reach the bridge. When they arrived, he told them that when he had first encountered them on Almas, he hadn't realized what a problem they would cause for the Empire. He then offered to give them quick and painless deaths if they dropped their weapons and surrendered to him. However, their conversation was interrupted, as Varth contacted Draco over a display screen, demanding to know why the Inquisitor hadn't powered down the station's tractor beams, to allow the Star Destroyer to participate in the battle and decimate the Alderaanian forces. Draco ordered his men to lower the tractor beams and was then immediately attacked by the Alderaanian agents. The agents killed Draco and the Resistance was able to succeed in destroying the Super Star Destroyer.[\[2\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-JOTS-2)

## Personality and traits\[ [Edit section: Personality and traits](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco?veaction=edit&section=7 "Edit section: Personality and traits")\]

" _I know that you've suffered great losses, that you wrestle with the same concerns of virtue and compromise that I did as a Jedi, the same concerns that have plagued the conscientious since time immemorial._"―Valin Draco[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

As a Jedi, Draco was heroic and idealistic, and at the start of the Clone Wars, he sided with the Republic. However, as the conflict drew on, he increasingly lost hope in what he was fighting for, and eventually completely abandoned his belief in the Republic after the Battle of Parein II 4.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

Draco was ruthless as well as calculating, and did not make rash judgments. He preferred to twist things so that they could serve his own means, rather than destroy them himself. This was demonstrated when he nursed Crant back to health and attempted to make Vhiin Thorla turn to the dark side of the Force. He also fully embraced his role as an Inquisitor, and was a skilled torturer. However, he viewed his allegiance to the Empire to be one of necessity, as it helped him to do what he wanted and he did not see himself as beholden to it. Despite this, he believed that being an Inquisitor was his destiny and that it had allowed him to find his true self.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5) By the time that Draco had traveled to Byss, he began to believe that he was future of the Empire and that he should exterminate any Jedi he met by engaging them in personal combat, like the Emperor had done. He planned to earn a high-ranking position within the New Order, by crushing the Alderaanian Resistance, which he believed would deter any future rebellions against the Empire.[\[2\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-JOTS-2)

Draco liked to seek revenge against people who had caused him harm or double-crossed him,[\[3\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-A_Wretched_Hive-3)[\[4\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-The_Queen_of_Air_and_Darkness-4) but was also wary of fighting those that he could not defeat, and as such, he knew when to retreat. An example of this was when he captured Denia aboard the _Resurgence_ and left without attacking a constant thorn in his side, the Alderaanian Resistance team,[\[13\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-ARoW-13) even though he had previously vowed to kill them.[\[4\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-The_Queen_of_Air_and_Darkness-4) Draco genuinely cared for Crant and was saddened when the night hunter was killed, although he tried not to show it.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

Draco was nearly two [meters](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Meter/Legends "Meter/Legends") in height[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5) and had black-colored [hair](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Hair/Legends "Hair/Legends") and fair skin.[\[3\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-A_Wretched_Hive-3) During his service as an Inquisitor, he was fitted with various cybernetic implants,[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) including a cybernetic thumb[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5) and eye.[\[6\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Sword_of_the_Empire-6)

## Powers and abilities\[ [Edit section: Powers and abilities](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco?veaction=edit&section=8 "Edit section: Powers and abilities")\]

Draco demonstrated various Force abilities, including Force lightning, Force Rage, [telekinesis](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Telekinesis/Legends "Telekinesis/Legends") and Beast Control. He also learned various techniques from Darth Rivan's holocron, such as how to create dreambeasts. The holocron significantly augmented Draco's connection to the Force and he was able to draw power from it, although he lost most of this after it was destroyed.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5) By the time of his encounter with the Alderaanian Resistance on Prakith, he had greatly grown in power since their previous encounter with him on Almas,[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) as the time he had spent in the Citadel Inquisitorius had infused him with the power of the dark side.[\[6\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Sword_of_the_Empire-6) After Denia sacrificed herself, she reduced his power, and he was no longer able to continue the fight.[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7)

Draco was multi-lingual—in addition to [Basic](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Galactic_Basic_Standard/Legends "Galactic Basic Standard/Legends"), he could also speak [Bocce](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bocce/Legends "Bocce/Legends"), [Dosh](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dosh/Legends "Dosh/Legends"), [High Galactic](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/High_Galactic_(language) "High Galactic (language)"), [Honoghran](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Honoghran "Honoghran"), and [Huttese](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Huttese/Legends "Huttese/Legends").[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

## Behind the scenes\[ [Edit section: Behind the scenes](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco?veaction=edit&section=9 "Edit section: Behind the scenes")\]

Valin Draco was a created as a recurring villain for _[Dawn of Defiance](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dawn_of_Defiance "Dawn of Defiance")_, a campaign produced by [Wizards of the Coast](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Wizards_of_the_Coast "Wizards of the Coast") to supplement the _[Star Wars Roleplaying Game](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Roleplaying_Game "Star Wars Roleplaying Game")_.[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) However, he received his first mention in the sourcebook [_Starships of the Galaxy_](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Starships_of_the_Galaxy_(Saga_Edition) "Starships of the Galaxy (Saga Edition)") in late [2007](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/2007 "2007"),[\[8\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-SotG-8) just prior to the release of _Dawn of Defiance_ in [2008](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/2008 "2008").[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) Draco also later received an entry in _[The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/The_Complete_Star_Wars_Encyclopedia "The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia")_[\[15\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-CSWE-15) and was indirectly mentioned in an adventure hook in the _[Rebellion Era Campaign Guide](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Rebellion_Era_Campaign_Guide "Rebellion Era Campaign Guide")_.[\[16\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-RECG-16)

In _[Echoes of the Jedi](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Echoes_of_the_Jedi "Echoes of the Jedi")_, the fourth scenario in the _Dawn of Defiance_ campaign, a number of different things involving Draco depend upon the actions of the players, who role-play as the Alderaanian Resistance agents. If the player characters are captured or incapacitated by Draco's Noghri Death Commandos, then they take them straight to Draco. Also, if the players confirm to Draco that it was they who killed Crant, then the glint in his eyes dulls, although he soon regains his composure. The players have the option to free Vhiin Thorla from his cage, and if they do, then he comes and fights Draco with them. If they do not know how to sever the Rivan gatekeeper from its holocron, then Thorla does this for them. Draco then snatches the holocron and throws it at Thorla, which makes it shatter and pushes Thorla off the edge of the chasm.[\[5\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Echoes_of_the_Jedi-5)

In _[Sword of the Empire](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Sword_of_the_Empire "Sword of the Empire")_, the ninth scenario in the _Dawn of Defiance_ campaign, it is possible for the player characters to encounter Draco either after infiltrating the Citadel Inquisitorius, or after being taken there if they are captured by Imperial forces.[\[6\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Sword_of_the_Empire-6) However, as the plot summarization of the scenario in _[Dawn of Defiance: Gamemaster Primer](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer "Dawn of Defiance: Gamemaster Primer")_ claims that the player characters encounter Draco after infiltrating the Citadel, this can be assumed to be the canonical option.[\[7\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Dawn_of_Defiance:_Gamemaster_Primer-7) The means by which Denia sacrifices herself during the events of _Sword of the Empire_ can also vary, depending upon player actions. If they succeed in inflicting a lot of damage on Draco and are about to defeat him, then he turns on Denia and attacks her with Force lightning, which she reflects back onto him. If, alternately, it seems that Draco is about to win, then Denia uses telekinesis to attack Draco and the resulting Force energy reflects back onto both of them. The _Gamemaster Primer_ claims that Draco was about to defeat the player characters, so this article assumes that it is that outcome that is the canonical one.[\[6\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-Sword_of_the_Empire-6)

The players again confront Draco in the final scenario of the campaign, _[Jaws of the Sarlacc](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jaws_of_the_Sarlacc "Jaws of the Sarlacc")_. If any of the player characters present during this encounter are Jedi, then Draco duels them and is prepared to fight to the death to defeat them, while relying on his Shadow Guards and stormtroopers to attack any non-Jedi player characters.[\[2\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-JOTS-2)

The release of the _[Legacy Era Campaign Guide](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Legacy_Era_Campaign_Guide "Legacy Era Campaign Guide")_—another supplement to the Star Wars Roleplaying Game—in [2009](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/2009 "2009") raised the possibility that Valin Draco is the ancestor of [Antares Draco](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Antares_Draco "Antares Draco"), an [Imperial Knight](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Knight "Imperial Knight") of the [Fel Empire](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Fel_Empire "Fel Empire"). While the _Legacy Era Campaign Guide_ does not explicitly state the familial bond, it is strongly implied in the entry for Antares Draco; it is noted that an ancestor of Antares was a member of the Inquisitorius under [Emperor](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Galactic_Emperor/Legends "Galactic Emperor/Legends") [Palpatine](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Palpatine/Legends "Palpatine/Legends").[\[17\]](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Valin_Draco#cite_note-LECG-17)
