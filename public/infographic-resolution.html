The Fulcrum
Action Resolution
Roll − Risk = Net Result
The Two Axes
🎯
Control
Your Discipline Die
“Did you succeed?”
Roll your Discipline die against the Risk (usually 4). Positive net = success. Negative net = failure.
Success — Net 0 or higher
Failure — Net −1 or lower
Mastery — Natural 8+ on die
💥
Power
Your Arena Die
“How hard did it hit?”
Your Arena die sets the impact. Higher roll = higher tier on the Effect Track. Favored disciplines explode on max roll.
Fleeting — Glancing / minimal
Masterful — Full intended effect
Legendary+ — Exceptional & beyond

⭐
Mastery Bonus
When the Control Die rolls a natural 8 or higher, the action resolves at +1 Effect Tier automatically. Applies to attacks, maneuvers, defenses, and narrative actions.
Tactical Levers
⬆
Pre-Roll
Dice Stepping
Step a die Up (D8→D10) or Down (D10→D8). Changes ceiling & floor before rolling.
[Optimized] steps up, [Disoriented] steps down
⇅
Post-Roll
Tier Shifting
Shift the final result +1 or −1 Tier on the Effect Track after seeing the roll.
Destiny = +1 Tier. Gambits cost −1 Tier for a bonus.
🎲
Post-Roll
Edge Reroll
Spend 1 Edge to reroll Control or Power die. Favored Disciplines only.
New result replaces the old — may be higher or lower.
🔥
Favored Disciplines — Gateway to Unleashed
Only Favored Disciplines can reach the Unleashed tiers. Your Power Die explodes on its max value — roll again and add. A D12 rolling 12 explodes to 12+, potentially reaching Unleashed I, II, or III. Favored also unlocks Edge rerolls.
