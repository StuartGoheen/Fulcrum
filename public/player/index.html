The Destiny Pool: Hope & Toll

2 tokens per player, any side up at campaign start. The ratio persists between sessions — it is a running ledger, not a mood ring.

The Karma State

The current ratio grants passive bonuses to social actions:

  • Hope dominant: +1 Tier on Charm/Persuasion vs. honorable or neutral contacts. Gap of 2+: −1 Tier on Intimidate/Deception (Soft Touch).
  • Toll dominant: +1 Tier on Intimidate/Deception vs. the underworld. Gap of 2+: −1 Tier on Charm/Persuasion (The Monster).

Tapping

Once per scene, after any roll, any player may tap one available token to increase the result by +1 Tier. Tapped tokens slide aside for the scene — they do not flip.

The Lockout

  • Toll → Hope: Only possible if the token is untapped. Tap a dark token to survive — the guilt of that method is sealed. A sacrifice later cannot redeem it.
  • Hope → Toll: Possible regardless of tap state. A dark deed taints the soul whether or not you were focused. A tapped Hope token that falls becomes a tapped Toll token immediately.

Flipping

  • Hope → Toll (The Fall): An act of unmitigated cruelty — executing a prisoner, torture, abandoning innocents, betrayal for credits.
  • Toll → Hope (Redemption): Accepting a severe, concrete disadvantage to stay clean — sparing a villain who will return, surrendering a major payday, deliberately failing an objective.

The Crossroads

When a declared action would trigger The Fall, the GM pauses before it resolves. Any crewmate may intervene out-of-turn by tapping an available Hope token and making a Social Maneuver against the acting player. The acting player then chooses:

  • Relent — Stand down. The Hope token was spent and the threat walks free. The crew flips one Toll → Hope.
  • Pull the Trigger — The plea is ignored. The Fall resolves. Flip one Hope → Toll. The intervening player's token stays tapped — they tried.
